Titration Simulation

Titration Experiment

Type: Acid-Base Reaction

Purpose: Determine concentration of an unknown base by neutralizing it with a known acid.

Equipment: Burette, Flask, Indicator, Acid/Base solutions

Steps:

  1. Fill burette with known acid.
  2. Place unknown base in flask with indicator.
  3. Slowly release acid drop by drop.
  4. Stop when solution turns color (neutralization).
